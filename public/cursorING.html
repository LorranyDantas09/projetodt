ABOUT THE COURSE

The Beginner Barber Course is the ideal gateway for those who wish to start a career in the world of barbering, even without any prior experience. In this course, the student learns from the basic fundamentals to the essential techniques that every good barber needs to master to work with confidence, professionalism, and creativity.

Finishing touches and final touches

Customer service and professional demeanor

Classic and modern fade haircuts, formal, military.
