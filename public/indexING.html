Our History

My name is Vanilson Silva, I am a businessman in the Barber Shop industry, since 2018. From a very young age, I worked to achieve my goals and dreams, I always had an entrepreneurial side, and it was in 2016 that a friend introduced me to a hairdressing course, where I found myself, discovered a passion that I didn't even know could exist. In 2018 I opened my barber shop in the city of Ocidental, all with a lot of work and dedication. Every detail thought out for quality service. After 6 months of work, we were recognized and won the award for best barber shop in the surrounding area by the company Top Marketing, competing with Jardim Ingá and Ocidental, then in 2022 we won the award for best barber shop in the city of Ocidental, and today, after 6 years, my company is structured, generating jobs and providing joy and self-esteem to our clients. I owe all this to myself for my persistence and determination to pursue my dream. There is still a long way to go. And I am grateful for every phase I have gone through and that I will still go through. Nice to meet you, I am Vanilson Silva, owner and proprietor of Detroit Barber Shop.

About Us

At Detroit Barber Shop, we go far beyond just a haircut. We specialize in presenting you with your best version, offering a complete experience of care, style, and well-being. With a highly qualified and passionate team, we combine technique, style, and personalized service for all audiences, delivering results that surprise and satisfy. Our space was created to provide comfort, confidence, and that moment just for you, with the best of traditional barbering and modern therapies.

Cut with care, attention, and respect

At our barbershop, we understand that each person is unique. That's why we offer a welcoming, adapted environment with professionals trained to serve children, teenagers, and adults on the autism spectrum. Here, time, patience, and comfort are a priority because more than just a haircut, we want to provide a positive and peaceful experience.

A Place Designed For You

Our barbershop offers a calm, airy, and welcoming environment, ideal for those on the autism spectrum seeking comfort during their service. The space was designed to reduce stimuli, with natural ventilation, soft lighting, and trained professionals who respect the time and needs of each client.

Special Care for Everyone

At Detroit Barber Shop, the space is designed to welcome and cater to children, women, and men. We offer a pleasant, airy, and comfortable environment where each client is treated with attention and respect. With skilled and caring professionals, we strive to guarantee a tranquil and personalized experience, valuing the style and needs of each person.
